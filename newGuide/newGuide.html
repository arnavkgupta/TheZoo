Habitat Guide
Spread Awareness Through Social Media
You can create artwork that showcases the beauty and importance of endangered species. You can use social media to showcase your artwork and raise awareness about endangered species. You can also share information about conservation efforts and ways that people can get involved.
Saving Endangered Species Youth Art Contest
This is an annual art competition organized by the Endangered Species Coalition, open to K-12 students in the US, with a different theme each year to create awareness on endangered species and their conservation. Winners get prizes and recognition.
Donate a Percentage of Profits to Conservation Organizations
If you sell your artwork, you can donate a percentage of the profits to organizations that work to protect endangered species. Some organizations you could donate to include the World Wildlife Fund, Wildlife Conservation Society, The Nature Conservancy, and the Rainforest Alliance.
The Endangered Species Print Project
This is an annual printmaking project that invites artists to create limited-edition prints of endangered species. The prints are sold to raise funds for conservation efforts. You can participate by submitting your artwork for consideration
The World Wildlife Fund's Annual Art Contest
WWF's annual art contest invites artists of all ages to create artwork inspired by wildlife and nature. The contest is open to artists from around the world and winners receive prizes and have their artwork showcased on WWF's website.
Run for Wildlife
This is an organization that hosts charity runs and walks to raise funds and awareness for conservation efforts. You can find events near you or participate in virtual events from anywhere in the world.
World Wildlife Fund's Panda Nation
This is a fundraising platform created by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) that allows individuals and teams to fundraise by participating in running and other fitness events.
Defenders of Wildlife's Wildlife Run
Defenders of Wildlife hosts an annual virtual 5k called the Wildlife Run. The event raises funds for the organization's work to protect wildlife and their habitats.
ConservationFIT
This is a virtual run that raises funds for the Wildlife Conservation Society. Participants can run, walk, or bike and raise funds for the organization's conservation efforts.
Virtual Running Club
This is an online community of runners who are passionate about conservation. The club hosts virtual events and challenges to raise funds and awareness for conservation organizations.
Host a Lifting Competition for Conservation
You can organize a lifting competition and donate the proceeds to a conservation organization that works to protect endangered species. You can partner with local gyms, businesses, and organizations to make the event a success.
